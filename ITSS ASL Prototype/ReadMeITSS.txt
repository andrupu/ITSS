===================
ITSS Sign Language
===================

Contents of the Program
------------------------
 - ITSS Prototype.html
 - LIH1N1(ASL).avi
 - ReadMeITSS.txt

How to Use
------------------------
 1. Open up  'ITSS Prototype.html'
 2. If blocked content notice comes up (bottom of web browser) click 'Allow blocked content' button
 3. Read the text presented
 4. If you want to see American Sign Language to the text click on 'Show Sign Language' button
 5. To close/ hide the Sign Language click on 'Hide Sign Language'
